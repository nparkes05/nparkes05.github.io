Website Evaluations

Zillow

Zillow is a real estate website. It provides details about avialable houses on the market for people to buy. It is one of the most visited real estate websites.

  • Contrast: There is not much contrast. They use black text with their blue icons on a off white background.
  • Repetition: In the menu section there is four links on the left of the logo and four on the right of the logo. In the body there is three sections all about real estate.
  • Alignment: Everything on the page is aligned around the center.
  • Proximity: The menu items are all located closely together. The search bar is in its on seperate space. Then the bottom three links are in their own section.
